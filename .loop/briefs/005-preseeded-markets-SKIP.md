# Brief: Pre-Seeded Markets from Bracket

**Status:** DEPRIORITIZED — not needed for Wednesday.

**Reason:** The audience won't know the agents well enough to bet strategically on future matchups. "Betting opens when the match is announced" is simpler and more fun for a casual crowd. The arena already auto-creates pools on match start, so spectators can bet as soon as a match is queued — no pre-seeding needed.

**If we revisit later:** When the bracket is generated, auto-create pools for Round 1. When a round completes, create pools for next round. This enables studying matchups and betting ahead — makes sense for repeat events where the audience knows the fighters.
